About Us

Ateneo Blue Repertory is the university's premier musical theatre organization, dedicated to bold, student-led storytelling on stage.

Vision

To be the premier college musical theater arts organization in the Philippines

To mold a holistic and disciplined community of artists in the spirit of the Ateneo's pursuit of magis

To bring about personal development and societal awareness through its productions

Mission

To train members in the fields of performance, production, and theatre management

To produce high-quality productions, projects, and services

To immerse constituents and audiences in shows that communicate a relevant message

To share talents and inspire creativity, joy, and imagination unique to musical theater

Our Services

Training

blueREP offers workshops in performance (music, acting, movement, improv), production (stage management, lighting/sound design, set/props, costume/makeup), and theatre management (Photoshop/design, logistics, documentation, external relations).

Gigs

Hire blueREP for lively performances at events, weddings, or company functions. Music and dance customized for the occasion. Same caliber as our mainstage productions.

Ushering

Need a Front-of-House team? blueREP's seasoned ushers are available for hire for your own shows or productions.
