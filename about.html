About Us

Ateneo Blue Repertory (blueREP) is the premier musical theatre organization of the Ateneo de Manila University.

Today, blueREP continues to hone its members in the fields of performance, production, and theatre management, aiming to mold a holistic and disciplined community of artists within the university. With passion, the organization endeavors to immerse its audiences in shows that communicate relevant messages, and to inspire in them creativity, joy, and imagination unique to musical theatre.
